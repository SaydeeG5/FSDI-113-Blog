Fabulous Las Vegas

Las Vegas is an internationally renowned major resort city, known primarily for its gambling, shopping, fine dining, entertainment, and nightlife. But this beautiful vibrant city has more to offer and more family friendly than its reputation . Come and see Las Vegas from our view .

Las Vegas Sign

Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a long-time Vegas vet, the welcome sign is a source of hope, happiness and good times. The feeling you get from seeing it is pure fabulous-ness!

The Venetian

If you can’t make it to Venice, Italy, the next best thing happens at the Venetian in Las Vegas. It’s a great outing with family/friends, or it can be a romantic date. There are two options when you get tickets for the gondola: an indoor ride or an outdoor ride. During the ride, the gondolier takes you through the canal and serenades you with.

Bellagio Fountains

Three cool things to do in spring/summer in Vegas. Two out of three are free! Join us , let's go see the Bellagio Botanical Garden , the famous Bellagio Fountains and have some icecream at the number one Cereal Bar in the World , Cereal Killerz Kitchen!

One of the best breakfast buffets is in Cosmopolitan Hotel, Wicked Spoon. Let's eat!

Lets check out New York New York , Hershey Store and M&M Store. If you are a chocolate lover these places are a must ! New York New York has a coney island style roller coaster!

What to do when its super hot? Cowabunga Water Park ! One of our favorite spots ; with fireworks , food trucks, DJs in the summer, of course thrilling water slides and vicious wave pool !

The biggest arcade in Vegas , Round 1 Bowling and Amusement. This is attached to a mall so win win for all of us. Hands down one of our favorite spots here in Vegas.

Las Vegas, home of the Golden Knights. Check out how we do here , join us , let's watch a game and parade. We made it on "fox5vegas" Instagram!

The Adventure Dome in Circus Circus This park has something for everyone, whether it’s exhilarating roller coasters, more sedate rides for kids, classic arcade games or cutting-edge VR adventures. The whole park is under a huge glass dome, so it’s open all year. We sure had an awesome time !

Bacchanal Buffet in Caesar's Palace. The best buffet in Vegas! Seafood, Steaks, food from all over the world and best desserts ! So worth it !

Omega Mart in Meow Wolf . It's an interactive, mind-bending immersive art experience. Explore an extraordinary supermarket that bursts into surreal worlds and unexpected landscapes.

Tournament of Kings in Excalibur is Las Vegas' ultimate dinner and a show experience. Adapted from the tale of King Arthur, valiant knights ride mighty steeds and prove their chivalry amongst special effects and pyrotechnics. While knights fight, guests feast on a hearty dinner using the original utensil – their hands. Tournament of Kings is an epic portrayal of centuries past, and it’s all in the name of honor, country, and of course, food.
